Why Reels Still Win in 2025 (And How to Make Yours Convert)

Selina Fan · 15 January 2025 · 6 min read

Short-form video has been the talk of the industry for years now — but in 2025, the game has shifted dramatically. The brands winning on Reels today aren't the ones making the most polished content. They're the ones who understand one fundamental truth: the algorithm rewards watch time, and watch time is earned in the first two seconds.

Why Reels Are Still the Highest-Leverage Format

Despite the noise about new platforms and formats, Instagram Reels continues to deliver the highest organic reach of any content type on Meta's properties. Our data across 30+ client accounts shows an average reach 3.2× higher for Reels compared to static posts — and that gap is widening, not closing.

But here's what most brands miss: reach alone means nothing. The question is always, what happens after the view?

The 2-Second Hook Formula

We've tested hundreds of hooks across our client accounts. The formats that consistently outperform are:

  • Leading with a bold, contrarian statement ("Most brands are doing this wrong.")
  • Opening mid-action — skip the title cards entirely
  • Addressing the viewer directly ("If you sell online, watch this.")
  • Creating visual contrast — something unexpected in the first frame

Conversion vs. Virality: Know Your Goal

One of the biggest mistakes brands make is optimising for views when they should be optimising for action. A Reel with 500K views that drives zero sales is not a success. A Reel with 12K views that generates 80 link-in-bio clicks and 15 DMs is.

"Virality is a vanity metric unless it's connected to a business outcome. Build your content strategy backwards from the outcome you want."

What We're Seeing Work Right Now

Across our client portfolio in Q1 2025, these content types are outperforming everything else:

  • Behind-the-scenes process videos — authentic, high watch time, builds trust
  • Founder/team POV content — human connection drives saves and shares
  • Educational "myth-busting" formats — high comment engagement and shares
  • Before/after transformations — works across e-commerce, beauty, fitness, food

The Takeaway

Reels aren't going anywhere. But the era of "just post a lot" is over. Every Reel needs a clear hook, a purpose, and a call to action — even if that CTA is just "save this for later." The brands growing fastest on Instagram in 2025 are the ones treating every piece of content as a micro-funnel.
